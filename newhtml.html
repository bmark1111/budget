How do Ejis shirts work?

We start with premium micro modal fabric as our foundation material. Micro modal was selected after an exhaustive process of testing dozens of different materials, none of which could match the silky feel, anti-microbial properties and water rendition ability of micro modal. By then adding 5% spandex it helps to add stretch that assists in keeping its shape and makes it feel like a high performance fabric than a regular undershirt.

A day doesn’t go by when we have a customer reach out to us to tell us how surprised they are with how soft their undershirt felt the first time they picked it up. It’s hard to get across how incredible it will feel on your skin when you put it on. You may end up hating all of your regular cotton shirts (sorry!).

The inner fabric layer of the pads is permanently fused with copper oxide which is a natural odor killer. Bacteria that thrive in moist environments like the underarm (and are the root cause of offensive body odor) are not able to multiply when they come into contact with copper.

Sweat is then blocked by a waterproof laminated fabric. While it stops all droplets from passing through it still breathes so you don’t overheat. This fabric is used in a number of demanding applications like hospitals and is able to stand up to repeated washing so it will never lose its stopping power.

Quite simply there is no other undershirt with this amount of technology, this level of high quality fabrics and ability to provide incredible comfort every time you wear it.

The end result is that your shirt is dry and you’re feeling more confident! That’s why we say that our undershirts are “wearable confidence”.

How do I care for my Ejis products?

All garments should be turned inside out and washed with cold water on a low cycle. Hang dry is recommended, but machine dry on a low heat is acceptable and won't damage the fabrics. Avoid bleach and high temperatures.
